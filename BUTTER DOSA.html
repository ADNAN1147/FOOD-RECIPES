Ingredient



  1. DOSA BATTER:


    • 2 cups rice
    • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
    • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    • Salt to taste

  2. BUTTER DOSA:


    • Butter for cooking dosa
    • Ghee
    • Idli podi or chutney powder

  3. COCONUT CHUTNEY:


    • 1 cup grated coconut
    • 2 green chilies
    • 1/2 inch ginger
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed


Instructions

DOSA BATTER

Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.


Grind them into a smooth batter, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.

COCONUT CHUTNEY

Grind grated coconut, green chilies, ginger, and salt with water to make a smooth chutney.

MAKING DOSA

Heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladle of dosa batter and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.


Drizzle ghee generously over the dosa. You can use the back of a spoon to spread the ghee evenly.


Cook the dosa until it is crisp and golden brown on one side.


Flip the dosa and cook the other side until it's done.

Enjoy your indulgent Butter Dosa!
