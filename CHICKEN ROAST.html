Ingredient



    • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil or melted butter
    • 1 tablespoon paprika
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 1 lemon, sliced
    • Fresh herbs (such as rosemary and thyme) for garnish


Instructions

Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).

Prepare the Chicken:

Clean and pat dry the whole chicken with paper towels. Make sure the chicken is at room temperature for even cooking.

Seasoning Mix:

In a small bowl, mix together paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme, dried rosemary, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.

Rub the Chicken:

Rub the chicken with olive oil or melted butter, ensuring it's well-coated. Sprinkle the seasoning mix over the entire chicken, rubbing it into the skin for even coverage.

Stuff with Lemon:

Place sliced lemons inside the chicken cavity. This adds flavor and helps keep the chicken moist.

Tie the Legs:

If desired, tie the chicken legs together with kitchen twine. This helps the chicken cook evenly.

Roasting:

Place the chicken on a roasting pan or a baking sheet with a wire rack. Roast in the preheated oven for approximately 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) and the skin is golden and crispy.

Baste Occasionally:

Optional: Baste the chicken with its juices or melted butter every 30 minutes to keep it moist.

Resting:

Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute, keeping the meat moist.

Garnish and Serve:

Garnish the chicken with fresh herbs like rosemary and thyme. Carve and serve with your favorite side dishes.

Enjoy your delicious and flavorful Chicken Roast!
