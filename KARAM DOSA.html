Ingredient



  1. DOSA BATTER:


    • 2 cups rice
    • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
    • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    • Salt to taste

  2. KARAM:


    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 2 tablespoons chana dal (split chickpeas)
    • 1 tablespoon urad dal (black gram lentils)
    • 2-3 dried red chilies
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1-inch ginger, grated
    • Salt to taste
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds

  3. COCONUT CHUTNEY:


    • 1 cup grated coconut
    • 2 green chilies
    • 1/2 inch ginger
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed


Instructions

DOSA BATTER

Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.


Grind them into a smooth batter, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.

KARAM

Heat oil in a pan. Add chana dal, urad dal, dried red chilies, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.


Add curry leaves, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute.


Allow the mixture to cool slightly and then grind it into a coarse paste. Add salt to taste.

COCONUT CHUTNEY

Grind grated coconut, green chilies, ginger, and salt with water to make a smooth chutney.

MAKING MASALA DOSA

Heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladle of dosa batter and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.


Drizzle ghee or oil around the edges of the dosa.


Spread a generous amount of the prepared Karam (spicy paste) evenly over the dosa.


Press the toppings lightly into the dosa with the spatula.


Cook until the dosa is crisp and golden brown.


Serve hot with coconut chutney or your favorite side dish.

Enjoy your flavorful and spicy Karam Dosa!
